Padi UMKM is an Indonesian platform for Improving the Productivity of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs). The program is designed to support the development of MSMEs in Indonesia by providing them with access to financing, technology, business training, and market linkages. The focus of the program is on enhancing the productivity and competitiveness of MSMEs in various sectors, including agriculture, manufacturing, and services.

The Problem

‚óè

The problem with the current transaction detail page on Padi UMKM's platform is that buyers frequently request revisions or changes to their transaction due to incorrect tax calculations that do not comply with their company policies. That problem is impacting the Bis-Ops and customer service teams, as well as the engineering resources needed to resolve the issue.

‚óè

Users are still having difficulty locating the detailed transaction history on the current page.

‚óè

Document section is located too far down the page, making it difficult for some users to locate the documents they are looking for.

‚óè

And when the transaction status changes, documents associated with the previous status are saved in the document section. The expectation is that after the revamp, documents associated with previous status should be saved but grouped together on one page and not filling up the entire section.

Old design of transaction detail page PaDi UMKM

Solution

‚óè

Redesign the transaction detail page to better highlight the important sections, such as the summary of the tax information, transaction history, and documents.

‚óè

Make the tax information more visible and prominent on the page to help sellers check the accuracy of their tax calculations before the buyer pays. This will help reduce complaints related to tax inconsistencies and ensure that sellers are following the correct tax policies.

‚óè

Place the section for transaction history and documents in a more accessible location, such as separate tab, to make it easier for sellers to find and manage their documents.

‚óè

Consider implementing a document pooling feature that groups all relevant documents together and allows sellers to access them from a single location, regardless of the transaction status. This will help ensure that all relevant documents are available to sellers, even if the transaction status changes.

Overall, the solution involves redesigning the detail transaction page to improve visibility, accessibility, and functionality, with a focus on reducing complaints related to tax inconsistencies and making it easier for sellers to manage their documents. By implementing these changes, the new design will help sellers check the accuracy of their tax calculations, reduce complaints, and increase overall user satisfaction.

Objective

‚óè

Reduce complaints related to discrepancies in tax details from the transaction detail page to customer service.

‚óè

Provide clear and concise information about tax details on the transaction detail page to help users understand the taxes associated with their transactions.

‚óè

Ensure that all documents related to an transaction are permanently documented, even if the transaction status changes.

‚óè

Make it easier for users to find information about their transaction history and understand it clearly and easily.

As a UI/UX designer, these objectives will guide my design decisions and help me to create a page that meets the needs of the users and solves the problems identified in the problem statement. I need to ensure that the design provides clear and concise information about tax details, makes it easy to find and manage documents, and is intuitive and user-friendly. Additionally, i should design the page in a way that minimizes complaints and increases user satisfaction by addressing the identified pain points.

The User & Audience

The users that are designing for in this project is sellers on Padi UMKM's platform. These sellers use the platform to sell their products and manage their transactions, so it's important that the detail transaction page provides them with all the information they need to fulfill their transactions and manage their businesses efficiently.

Role & Responsibility

My role in this project is UI/UX designer, i create a new and improved transaction detail page for Padi UMKM's platform. This involves understanding the needs of the users (in this case, the sellers), analyzing the current user flow and pain points, and using this information to design a page that is intuitive, user-friendly, and efficient.

Scope

The scope for the new transaction detail page in this project is a web/desktop page.
